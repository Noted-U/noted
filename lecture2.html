Neuroanatomy
Planes of section
  • Sagittal cut
    • Median cut dividing two hemispheres (left & right)
    • Midsagittal
    • Parasagittal
  • Frontal cut
    • Coronal cut dividing anterior and posterior parts at plane of the face
  • Transverse/Axial/Horizontal cut
    • Longitudinal axis: parallel to the rostral-caudal axis of the brainstem and spinal cord (vertical)
    • Transverse axis: perpendicular to the RC axis
  • Oblique cut
    • NONE of the three planes above
